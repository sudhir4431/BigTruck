A LITTLE STORY ABOUT US

Operating since 2017, Bigtruck a Division of Bigtruck Technologies Private Limited headquartered in Bengaluru in Karnataka, India is a professionally managed company engaged in providing end to end IT based services for road transportation businesses. Our interactive mobile application provides all possible logistics solutions for facilitating road transportation and cargo shipment across India.


Bigtruck solutions are built in a way that allows all business needs and activities to be integrated in a seamless technology that scales to meet the unique needs of every supply chain. We endeavour to provide every kind of business and enterprise with a viable solution to meet their logistics management needs by providing software solutions and customising them according to the client's needs.


We've applied science and technology to optimise the logistics and supply chain processes to deliver ROI for our customers. The company also boasts to have a wonderful dedicated team which is fully trained and conversant with all aspects of transport and technology.
